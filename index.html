car axle client

like a games website, but better.

Features

Games loader

A cool trick to allow you to play games on most websites. car axle client currently has a growing library of 20+ games for you to enjoy.

Exploits

car axle client has neat little tricks that make your life easier. One of them is Force Select, it allows selection on disabled elements

Pocket Browser

Browse through restrictions. Need to look something up but your teacher doesn't like that? Just use car axle client :).

Youtube Viewer

Watch youtube anywhere. Just paste the id, as simple as that! Now you can not pay attention in class in a whole new way.

Trustworthy

Community

car axle client has 25+ stars on github as well as a discord server you can join.

Open Source

Want to know how car axle client works? Just read the code or even build the version yourself!

FAQ

How do I request features?

Go to the github and create an issue with the feature request template or dm/add me on discord (@penguinify)

Can I fork/take your code?

car axle client is under GPL3, which means, yes, you can. But if you do please give credit, I don't really make that much money of this.

How do I update?

When there is a update, car axle client will give you a notification. It will give you a link to reinstall car axle client.

Will more games added?

Maybe, maybe not. I am currently focusing on the exploits side of car axle client and there are no future plans to add more games.
